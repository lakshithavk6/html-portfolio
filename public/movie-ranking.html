THE BEST MOVIES ACCORDING TO LAKSHITHA

MY TOP 3 MOVIES OF ALL TIME

Inception

A mind-bending sci-fi thriller where dreams are used as a weapon to steal secrets. It blurs the line between reality and imagination in the most gripping way.

3 Idiots

A heartfelt comedy-drama about friendship, pressure, and finding your true passion. It questions the education system while making you laugh and think.

The Dark Knight

A gritty superhero film that dives deep into chaos, morality, and justice. The intense face-off between Batman and the Joker is unforgettable.
